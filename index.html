About Us

KWTD is a platform which connects students, job seekers, and individuals at any professional stage with experts and mentors by using our inbuilt AI algorithm to receive career guidance and counselling. Our goal is to ensure that we connect and build a community which includes every kind of professional help. We aim to provide our community members with the right direction and support to achieve their career goals in today’s dynamic and ever-changing job market. Mentors of KWTD will be experienced, recognized and certified in their respective careers. KWTD aims to make your journey beautiful as our mentors will guide, empathize and understand your concerns with patience.

Services

Career Counselling

Our mentors will provide one-to-one coaching sessions in different fields with various expertise. Industry experts in finance, marketing, digital, literature, music, contemporary art and more will be one click away to provide you with career counselling. Our mentors will identify your strengths, weaknesses and opportunities in your chosen field and summarize the right guidance.

Master Classes

The industry experts will provide you with the opportunities to attend their personalized masterclasses which will cover topics regarding skill development, career development and professional growth.

Networking

Networking with our mentors and other learners in the community will provide you with high engagement and insights. It will help you expand your network to gain better opportunities in your career.
